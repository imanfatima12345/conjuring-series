The Conjuring Series is a chilling collection of horror films inspired by real paranormal cases of Ed and Lorraine Warren, filled with haunted houses, cursed objects, and demons that'll make you question every creak in the dark.

Ed and Lorraine Warren were a husband and wife duo of paranormal investigators, he a demonologist, she a clairvoyant who tackled some of the world's most chilling hauntings, inspiring The Conjuring Films.The left picture is of The Warrens from the movies and the right one is of them from real life.

The Evil that haunts them all

Valak: A high ranking demon from the depths of hell, taking the haunting form of a sinister nun.Clever, manipulative, and relentless, Valak preys on fear, using holy imagery as a twisted disguise to get closer to its victims.


Malthus: A malevolent demon bound to the cursed Anabelle doll, using it as a vessel to spread chaos. Patient and cunning, Malthus doesn't just haunt, it lures, manipulates, and destroys from within the shadows.


Bathsheba Sherman:The vengeful spirit of a 19th century witch who cursed her land and all who lived on it. Twisted by hatred, she seeks to possess mothers and claim the souls of their children.


La llorona:The weeping woman of legend, doomed to wander the earth after drowning her own children. Her mournful cries lure the living, but those who hear them are marked for death.


Crooked Man:A nightmarish, contorted figure born from a twisted nursery rhyme. With an unnerving grin and unnatural movements, he stalks his victims in a terrifying game of rhyme and fear.


The Occultist:A sinister human witch who serves dark forces, using forbidden rituals to summon demons and curse her victims. Ruthless and cunning, she blurs the line between mortal and monster.
